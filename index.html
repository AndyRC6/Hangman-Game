Greetings Adventurer! In order to prove your might to the king, you must slay all those who oppose the kingdom. Use your magical letter runes against your opponent. If the enemy is weak against that letter, it will damage them. If you guess the entire word that their weaknesses form, you will defeat them! But if you guess wrong, your attack will backfire and hurt you, so be careful - you have a limited number of hitpoints before you die.


controls:

You can use your letter runes by pressing any letter key on your keyboard, or by clicking the letter runes in the rune tab.

After you've defeated an enemy, press enter on your keyboard to fight the next opponent. If you manage to get yourself killed, press enter to start a new game.

You can change the level by clicking the icons in the "choose your level" panel. This will not affect gameplay in any way.



Hard mode:

If you choose to play on hard mode, your hp will not replenish between enemies. Your death is imminent.
